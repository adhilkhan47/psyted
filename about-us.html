The Story Of Psyted

A journey through purpose and perception.

At Psyted, we believe that mental well-being isn’t a destination it’s a continuous journey of connection, sharing, and growth. Each person’s story is different, yet we all share the same need to be seen, heard, and understood. That belief forms the heart of everything we do.

Our journey began in Kerala with a simple intention: to make mental-health support accessible, compassionate, and culturally rooted. We noticed that while conversations about physical health were common, discussions about emotional well-being were often hidden behind silence or stigma. Psyted was created to change that—to bring understanding and openness into everyday life, in a language and spirit that feels familiar to our community.

From our earliest days, we started small—with awareness programs and workshops that encouraged people to explore their emotions, ask questions, and support one another. One of our first initiatives, “മനസറിഞ്ഞ് (Manasarinju)”, brought mental-health awareness into schools, helping children learn to recognize their feelings and build emotional resilience. These sessions reminded us that change begins when we listen with empathy and respond with care.

As our community grew, so did our vision. We began hosting holistic wellness experiences—like our two-day retreat in the hills, where participants could disconnect from daily noise and reconnect with themselves. Through guided reflections, nature walks, and heartfelt sharing, these gatherings became spaces of healing and belonging.

Today, Psyted stands as more than just a mental-health platform. It’s a growing community of therapists, educators, and individuals who believe that emotional health is a collective effort. We continue to evolve alongside the people we serve—listening to their stories, learning from their needs, and building services that meet them where they are.

Our story is still unfolding, shaped by every conversation, every act of courage, and every connection we make. Because at Psyted, we don’t just talk about mental health—we live the journey of connecting, sharing, and evolving together.

Journey Was Started

A small idea, a big purpose — Psyted is born to make mental-health support accessible for Malayali people, combining local cultural understanding with online convenience.

First batch of online therapists onboarded
September 2019

We onboarded our first panel of Malayali psychologists so people could book sessions in Malayalam and English.

Launched “മനസറിഞ്ഞ്” school outreach
October 2020

Our first school program to build awareness about childhood mental health and emotional literacy.

First World Mental Health Day community event
October 2021

A community celebration with workshops, short talks and creative activities to destigmatise mental health.

Launched child-and-adolescent counselling service
June 2022

Dedicated services for children and teens, with age-appropriate programs and school partnerships.

“Your Mate” suicide-prevention initiative
September 2023

Community boxes, helpline info drives and campus visits to increase help-seeking among youth.

March 2024

Our community retreat focusing on mindfulness, group sharing and practical tools for daily wellbeing.

Platform growth milestone
November 2025

Reached a major usability milestone: expanded specialist panels, more appointment slots and local community programs.
